Platform Policy

Application Requirement & Eligibility Policy

A secure, fair, and streamlined rental process designed to support every applicant — not screen them out.

Effective Date: April 15, 2026

Overview

To ensure a secure, fair, and streamlined rental experience for all applicants, the following application guidelines apply to every property listed on our platform. This is a standard platform-wide policy — it is not set per property and applies equally to every listing on Choice Properties.

Our process is designed to be supportive, transparent, and tenant-first. We believe every applicant deserves a clear, guided path to finding their next home.

1. Apply First — Always

All prospective tenants are required to complete a formal application for a property before proceeding to any additional steps. This includes, but is not limited to, property inquiries, reservations, or viewing considerations.

This process ensures that all applicants are properly reviewed and given a fair opportunity to move forward. It also protects your time — you'll always know exactly where you stand.

2. Application Fee

Each application must be submitted along with the applicable application fee as clearly displayed on the property listing. This fee covers the cost of processing, verification, and evaluation of your application.

The application fee:

  • Is displayed on the listing and confirmed before you pay
  • Is non-refundable once your application is submitted and payment is processed
  • Covers administrative processing — not a reservation or approval guarantee

For more details, see the Application Credit Policy.

3. Fair Opportunity — Re-Application Benefit

We believe every applicant deserves a real chance to find a home. That's why if your application is not approved, you will be granted the opportunity to submit up to two (2) additional applications for other properties on the platform without being required to pay an additional application fee.

This ensures that a single outcome never closes the door on your search. You can keep looking, keep applying, and keep moving forward.

4. Structured, Guided Process

Our application-first approach is designed to protect both applicants and property providers by ensuring that only serious and qualified inquiries proceed through the system. Every step following your application will be clearly guided, with full transparency and support provided throughout.

Once your application is submitted:

  1. You receive an email confirmation with your Application ID
  2. Our team reviews your application, typically within 24 to 72 business hours
  3. You will be notified by email when your status changes
  4. Track your application status anytime using the Track My Application portal

Application Statuses — What They Mean

StatusWhat it means
Received — Pending ReviewYour application is in the queue
Under ReviewYour application is actively being reviewed
ApprovedCongratulations — proceed to your lease and holding deposit
Not SelectedThis application was not approved — your re-application benefit applies
WaitlistedYou are held as an alternate in case the primary applicant does not proceed
WithdrawnYou or the platform withdrew this application

Co-Applicants

One co-applicant may be included on a single application at no additional charge. Both applicants agree to the same terms and are evaluated together as a household. All applicants must be 18 years of age or older.

Questions?

If you have questions about this policy or your application, contact our support team at support@choiceproperties.com. We're here to help at every step.
