- Footer or global “Terms Preview” section (short version with link to full policy)

This is a STANDARD PLATFORM-WIDE RULE and should NOT be editable per property.

POLICY TITLE:
Application Requirement & Eligibility Policy

POLICY CONTENT:

To ensure a secure, fair, and streamlined rental experience for all applicants, the following application guidelines apply to every property listed on our platform:

1. Mandatory Application Before Any Next Step  
All prospective tenants are required to complete a formal application for a property before proceeding to any additional steps. This includes, but is not limited to, property inquiries, reservations, or viewing considerations.  
This process ensures that all applicants are properly reviewed and qualified before moving forward.

2. Application Fee Requirement  
Each application must be submitted along with the applicable application fee as clearly displayed on the property listing.  
This fee covers the cost of processing, verification, and evaluation of the applicant’s information.

3. Fair Opportunity Policy (Re-Application Benefit)  
In the event that an application is not approved, the applicant will be granted the opportunity to submit up to two (2) additional applications for other properties on the platform without being required to pay an additional application fee.  
This ensures that every applicant is given a fair and extended opportunity to secure a suitable property.

4. Structured Process Assurance  
Our application-first approach is designed to protect both applicants and property providers by ensuring that only serious and qualified inquiries proceed through the system.  
Each step following the application will be clearly guided, with full transparency and support provided throughout the process.
